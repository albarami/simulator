## SLIDE 6: RISK MITIGATION

### How We Minimize Downside

**1. Phased Approach**
- Start with low-risk employer-paid
- Decision gates after each phase
- Can pause/adjust if issues arise

**2. Demand Monitoring**
- Weekly tracking first 90 days
- Automatic alerts if >10% drop
- Fast response capability

**3. Political Safeguards**
- Waiver program for workers <1,500 QAR/month
- Conditional pricing (government exempt)
- Strong messaging campaign
- 45-day public notice periods

**4. Stakeholder Engagement**
- Chamber of Commerce consultation
- HR associations briefing
- Media pre-briefing
- Customer support readiness

**Expected Demand Impact:** 7% average drop (well within acceptable range)
